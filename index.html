About

Peter spends most of his time planning, managing and executing software implementation and development projects for healthcare organizations and private practices.

He served as a battle paramedic in the South African artillery before earning degrees in Physiotherapy and Medical Science. After practicing Physiotherapy as an Orthopedic Specialist for 11 years, he was invited to express a side interest and aptitude for computing by implementing clinical systems full time.

Experience in the technology arena led to further interests in business and project management, an MBA through the University of Edinburgh and PMP certification..

Peter continues to integrate and express all of his skills through current endeavors, performing systems analysis, consulting, development, coordination and management. His greatest strengths are bridging the gaps between business leaders, clinical end users and technical staff to build effective teams, translate strategic requirements into technical plans and execute.

He is happiest managing multiple, meaningful projects concurrently and vigorously toward realization of strategic objectives.
